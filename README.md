## 🔥 What is Reflex Rush?

Reflex Rush is a **browser-based reaction-time trainer** that measures and tracks exactly how fast your brain can spot a target and your hand can fire a click. No installs. No downloads. Just raw reflexes.

Bright red targets explode onto a dark canvas — **click them as fast as humanly possible.** Your reaction time is logged down to the millisecond, every single hit.

---

## 🎮 Game Modes

| Mode | Goal | Score |
|------|------|-------|
| 🎯 **Sprint** | Click **10 targets** as fast as possible | Lowest total time wins |
| ⚡ **Blitz** | Click as many targets as you can in **60 seconds** | Highest count wins |
| 💪 **Endurance** | Click **25 targets** without losing pace | Lowest total time wins |

---

## 📊 What Gets Tracked

Every session records:

- **Total time** — your end-to-end run duration
- **Reaction time per target** — from the moment it appears to the moment you click
- **Average reaction time** — your mean across the whole run
- **Best & worst reaction** — your peak and your slip
- **A full per-click bar chart** — color-coded by speed so you can see exactly where you slowed down

> 🟢 **< 280ms** — Exceptional &nbsp;|&nbsp; 🟡 **< 450ms** — Good &nbsp;|&nbsp; 🟠 **< 700ms** — Average &nbsp;|&nbsp; 🔴 **700ms+** — Keep practicing

## 🏆 Local Leaderboard

Your scores are **saved locally in your browser** — no account needed. Every run is ranked, and the leaderboard keeps your top 20 scores per mode so you're always chasing your own personal best.

Hit a new record and a **🏆 NEW BEST!** badge celebrates it on the results screen.

## 🚀 How to Play

1. **[Open the game](https://kyle5507.github.io/cursor-reflex-test/)**
2. Pick a mode
3. Click anywhere to start
4. Hit every **red box** the instant it appears
5. Check your stats — then try to beat them
